Strategic Decision-Making Framework

As a CEO, your decision-making process directly impacts your organization's success. This module introduces the DECIDE framework: a systematic approach to making strategic decisions under pressure while considering multiple stakeholders.

The DECIDE Framework

  • Define the problem clearly and precisely
  • Establish criteria for evaluating solutions
  • Consider alternatives and brainstorm options
  • Identify the best alternative based on criteria
  • Develop and implement an action plan
  • Evaluate and monitor the solution's effectiveness

Research shows that executives who follow structured decision-making processes achieve 25% better outcomes compared to those who rely solely on intuition. The key is balancing analytical thinking with executive intuition.
